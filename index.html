In Pakistan, the utilization of social media among the youth demographic is experiencing a significant surge, with various platforms competing for user engagement. However, there exists a notable deficiency in comprehensive data concerning the most prevalently used social media platform within this demographic. This knowledge gap poses a challenge in the formulation of effective digital engagement strategies.

The optimal outcome would involve obtaining a clear understanding of the most frequently used social media platform by Pakistani youth. Furthermore, acquiring insights into the factors influencing their platform preferences would facilitate the development of more targeted and impactful digital strategies and policies


The research primarily employed quantitative and mixed methods approaches
